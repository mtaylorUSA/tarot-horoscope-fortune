## Image Generation Prompt — Style Anchored

> **IMAGE GENERATION PROMPT — STYLE ANCHORED**
>
> Please generate a single zodiac icon image in the **exact visual style** of the two reference images I have attached.
>
> **STYLE REQUIREMENTS (MANDATORY):**
> - Engraved / etched **emblem style**, not illustrative or cartoon
> - Flat **gold line art** on a deep royal blue background
> - Minimal facial expression (neutral, symbolic, not emotive)
> - No painterly shading, no gradients, no soft rendering
> - Design should look like it could be **stamped, cast, or engraved** in metal
> - Strong symmetry and restrained detail
> - Symbolic abstraction over character realism
>
> **DO NOT:**
> - Do not make the image look cute, animated, or cartoonish
> - Do not add expressive eyes, smiles, or human emotion
> - Do not use modern illustration styles
> - Do not add text labels or typography
> - Do not copy or reference any specific tarot deck or artist
>
> **CONTENT REQUIREMENTS:**
> - Subject: **[INSERT ZODIAC SIGN NAME HERE]**
> - Include the zodiac’s **ancient astrological glyph** (public-domain symbol), rendered in the same gold engraved style
> - Glyph should be integrated subtly into the composition (e.g., chest, halo, base, or emblem), not as text
>
> **COMPOSITION:**
> - Centered emblem
> - Consistent scale with the reference images
> - Decorative stars are acceptable but should be minimal and secondary
>
> **LEGAL / ETHICAL CONSTRAINTS:**
> - This must be an original artwork
> - Do not reproduce or closely imitate any copyrighted tarot deck
> - Use only public-domain symbolism rendered in an original visual expression
>
> The final image should visually match the **level of abstraction, restraint, and engraved quality** of the attached reference images, not drift toward illustration or character art.

## Notes

- This prompt is intentionally strict to prevent style drift.
- If results begin to look illustrative, reiterate:
  - “More engraved / emblematic”
  - “Less illustrative, more symbolic”
- Keep this file as the **single source of truth** for zodiac icon generation.
